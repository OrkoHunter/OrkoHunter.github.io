About me

Hello! My name is Himanshu Mishra. I am a Software Engineer at Mercari US, living in Tokyo, Japan. I graduated from IIT Kharagpur, India.

I spent my college days discovering my affection for Software engineering. I feel the same way about creating software as I do for creating any kind of art or literature. A few of my open source projects have ended up with a small community around them. I spend a lot of time advocating Open Source to people around me.

I have an affection for literature. Reading and Writing are in my weekly routine. I publish my blog posts on this website. They are categorized as Tech or Non-Tech or sometimes both. Apart from blog posts, I also write letters and make some of them public.
